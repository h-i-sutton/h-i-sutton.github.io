![Flag](/images/flag_USA.jpg) ![Flag](/images/flag_russia.jpg) My hardback copy of Larry Bond’s latest Jerry Mitchell submarine novel, Arctic Gambit, has arrived. Expect a book review when I’ve read it cover to cover.   

This book is of special interest because the story centers on a new Russian first strike weapon based on the [Poseidon](http://www.hisutton.com{% post_url 2018-03-30-Status-6_KANYON %}) (Status-6 / Skif / KANYON) nuclear-armed nuclear-powered intercontinental torpedo. That’s reason enough to read this book.   
![Flag](/images/LarryBond_ArcticGambit.jpg)  

Larry Bond is suitably experienced and superbly well informed, with a enviable network of contacts, so expect a very insightful work with a streak of realism.   

Get your copy on [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Arctic-Gambit-Jerry-Mitchell-Novel/dp/0765334925)
