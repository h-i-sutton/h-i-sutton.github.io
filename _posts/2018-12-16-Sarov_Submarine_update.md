![Flag](/images/flag_russia.jpg) OSINT: **Russia's Sarov Class special test submarine in shed**  
Sometimes it is what is missing rather than what is present that is interesring in a satelite image. The special purpose test submarine [Pr.20120 Sarov](http://www.hisutton.com{% post_url 2019-02-22-SAROV-Class_Submarine %}) is inextricably linked to the [Poseidon](http://www.hisutton.com{% post_url 2019-02-22-Poseidon_Torpedo %}) (KANYON) Intercontinental Nuclear-Powered Nuclear-Armed Autonomous Torpedo. The boat spends almost all of the time moored in Severodvinsk in the vicinity of 64°35'10.42"N, 39°49'18.63"E. Unusually, the boat was out of the water in October 2018. One possibility is that she is being further modified to continue tests of the new weapon.
