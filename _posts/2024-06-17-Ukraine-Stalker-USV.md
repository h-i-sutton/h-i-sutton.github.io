# Ukraine's New USV, The Stalker 5.0

![Flag Ukraine](/images/flag_ukraine.jpg) The Stalker 5.0 USV was revealed at the international Black Sea Security Forum 2024 in Odesa. The type is described as being intended for reconnaissance and patrol, and can also be used for transporting supplies. Unit price is stated as 60k USD, making it cheaper than some other designs.  

![STALKER 5.0 USV](/images/Ukraine-USV-Stalker-profile.jpg)  

It is approximately 5 meters long and 1.2 meters wide, and carries a 150kg payload. The vessel uses a 60 hp outboard motor to achieve maximum speeds of 40 knots. Range is 350-600 km.

![STALKER 5.0 USV](/images/Ukraine-USV-Stalker.jpg)
