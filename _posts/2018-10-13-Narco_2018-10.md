**Narcosub intercepted by El Salvador**  
2nd October 2018  
![Narcosubs - Covert Shores](/images/Narcosub_20181002.jpg)  
El Salvador has intercepted a narcosub in Eastern Pacific Ocean, 450 nautical miles south of the Port of Acajutla, Sonsonate, on 2nd October 2018. The vessel is from the Converted Speedboat LPV (Low Profile vessel) lineage and is almost identical to many previous craft (see above!). It had four outboard motors and was carrying 2 tonnes of cocaine in 100 packages, each consisting of 20 packs of approximately one kilogram of cocaine. Three Colombians were arrested aboard the vessel. Ref [elsalvador.com](https://www.elsalvador.com/fotogalerias/noticias-fotogalerias/527573/el-narco-submarino-con-mayor-cargamento-de-droga-interceptado-en-el-salvador/)
