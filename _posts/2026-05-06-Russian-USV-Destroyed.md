![Flag Russia](/images/flag_russia.jpg) A Russian surface drone (USV – uncrewed surface vessel) has been destroyed outside a Ukrainian port by the 1st Company Group of the Ukrainian 801st Combat Divers Centre. The Special Forces operators used a quadcopter FPV (first person view) drone to strike the USV as it approached.

Analysis of the [video recorded by the FPV drone](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DX8pM_FNY69/) confirms that the USV is a previously seen type revealed in August 2025. We do not yet have a reported name for the Russian type, but it fits with expectations.
