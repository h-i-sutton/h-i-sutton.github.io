# New Swimmer Delivery Vehicles (SDVs) From UAE

![Flag](/images/flag_UEA.jpg) ![Flag](/images/flag_sweden.jpg) UAE (/Sweden) based Arma-Craft, a boat builder specializing in composite vessels, have [reportedly](https://www.navalnews.com/naval-news/2021/02/armacraft-sign-sizable-order-for-high-speed-diver-delivery-craft/) signed a substantial order for its new Trident SDVs. The submersible boat design is marketed as a submersible high-speed diver delivery craft. The exact model of craft (see Mk.I and Mk.II below) and Foreign Navy customer have not been released.

## Trident Mk.I

A catamaran design reminiscent of the [SubSeaCraft Diver Delivery Unit]({% post_url 2017-10-17-SubSeaCraft_DDU %}), the Mk.I can carry six divers.   

![Trident Mk1 catamaran SDV](/images/ArmaCraft-Trident-mk1.jpg)  

As-of November 2020 the proptotype was under construction.

## Trident Mk.II

![Trident Mk.2 catamaran SDV](/images/ArmaCraft-Trident-mk2.jpg)  

The monohull Mk.II is a larger craft capable of carrying 12 divers.
