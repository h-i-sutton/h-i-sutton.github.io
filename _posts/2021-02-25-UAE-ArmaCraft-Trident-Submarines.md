#New Swimmer Delivery Vehicles (SDVs) From UAE![Flag](/images/flag_UEA.jpg) ![Flag](/images/flag_sweden.jpg) UAE (/Sweden) based Arma-Craft, a boat builder specializing in composite vessels, have [reportedly](https://www.navalnews.com/naval-news/2021/02/armacraft-sign-sizable-order-for-high-speed-diver-delivery-craft/) signed a substantial order for its new Trident SDVs. The submersible boat design is marketed as a submersible high-speed diver delivery craft. The exact model of craft (see Mk.I and Mk.II below) and Foreign Navy customer have not been released.
