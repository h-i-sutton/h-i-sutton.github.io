#Russian Submarine Russian Belgorod Seen In Western White Sea![Flag Russia](/images/flag_russia.jpg) The latest Russian submarine launched for several decades, K-329 Belgorod, [sailed on sea trials on June 25](https://www.navalnews.com/naval-news/2021/06/russias-gigantic-submarine-belgorod-sails-for-the-first-time/). Today the vessel remains in the Western White Sea and was caught on the surface (no great surprise) by a passing Sentinel 2 satellite.   

Radar (SAR) satellite image from Sentinel 1, same vicinity. Measures ~170m length so strong likihood it's Belgorod:
