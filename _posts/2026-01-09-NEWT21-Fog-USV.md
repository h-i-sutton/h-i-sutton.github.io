The craft has similar overall dimensions to the classic Ukrainian Magura V5 USV, but is somewhat smaller due to the barrower beam of the main hull. Large wing-like sponsons at the stern give it a similar overall bream to the Magura however.




![Ukrainian USV Fog - Latvia](/images/Ukraine-Latvia-Newt21-USV-202601-1-940.jpg)  
![Ukrainian USV Fog - Latvia](/images/Ukraine-Latvia-Newt21-USV-202601-940.jpg)  


**Specifications**
Length: 4.5 m
Width: 1.5 m
Gross weight: < 200 kg
Payload capacity: up to 200 kg
Speed: 27 knots (50 km/h) cruising, 42 knots (78 km/h) max.
Power: 90 hp motor driving a waterjet, with 30-200 litres of fuel
Range at cruising speed: up to 485 nautical miles (900 km)
