#Russia Shows New Naval Drones At FLOT 2024 ShowRussian manufacturers have shown off a range of uncrewed naval platforms at the Flot 2024 show in St. Petersburg. While these are partly for export, the shadow of the war in the Black Sea is not far away.   

##ASV-1000The ASV-1000 is advertised as a multirole USV. It is slightly larger than typical USVs used by Ukraine in the Black Sea, with a cabin-like superstructure. Payload is significant at 1,000 kg.  

![USV ASV 1000](/images/Russia-USV-ASV1000.jpg)  

**ASV 1000 Specifications**   
Length: 6.2 m  
Beam: 2.3 m  
Maximum speed: 40 knots   
Range: 500+ km   
Payload: 1,000 kg   

##Orcan USVThe Orcan USV is presented as a basic platform for robotic marine systems. It is similar in size to the USVs used by Ukraine and has a single waterjet drive.   
![USV Orcan](/images/Russia-USV-Orcan.jpg)  

**Orcan USV Specifications**   
Length: 5.3 m  
Beam: 1.7 m  
Height: 0.8 m  
Payload: 100 liters   

![USV Orcan](/images/Russia-USV-Orcan-details.jpg)  

##MMT-300 AUVSmall autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV).   
![USV Orcan](/images/Russia-AUV-MMT-300.jpg)  

**MMT-300 AUV Specifications**   
Length: 2.7 m  
Diameter: 0.29 m  
Operating depth: 300 m  
Range: 3.5 km   

##Talisman-2 ROVWork-class remote operated vehicle (ROV).  
![ROV-Talisman](/images/Russia-ROV-Talisman-2.jpg)  

##MPA ROVThe 'MPA' small-sized ROV from *JSC GIPP Region* is designed for search, classification, designation of underwater objects. And also carrying out underwater technical work. The obvious context is mine countermeasures (MCM) and it appears similar to some western mine-neutralizers.   
![MPA ROV](/images/Russia-ROV-MPA.jpg)  
Modular design allows configurations including a search module (presumably with side-scan sonar), or a work module with an electromechanical manipulator.  

##ISPUM-EThe ISPUM-E complex from *JSC GIPP Region* is designed to search and destroy (or mark) sea mines. The system is intended for dedicated Pr.12700 Alexandrite Class mine countermeasures (MCM) ships. The system is not new and has been displayed before.   
![ISPUM-E](/images/Russia-ROV-ISPUM-E.jpg)  
![ISPUM-E](/images/Russia-MCM-Alexandrite.jpg)
