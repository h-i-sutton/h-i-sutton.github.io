**Revealing video of Pr.885M 'Kazan' launch**   
![Flag](/images/flag_russia.jpg) A video has emerged of the latest Russian **Project 885M** YASEN-M Class cruise missile submarine ‘Kazan’ being launched in April.   

Link to video (Russian): [YOUTUBE](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2XftAtr4mhw)
