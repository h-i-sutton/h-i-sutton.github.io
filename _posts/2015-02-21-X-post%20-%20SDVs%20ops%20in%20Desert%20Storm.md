![](/images/flag_USA.jpg) This interesting article appeared on a USN SEALs related blog that I follow. Retired SEAL Michael Wood gives a first-hand account of a close encounter during SDV ops as part of Operation Desert Storm.   
  
"*I heard a tune playing in my head from the Jaws movie, Da dunt… Da dunt… Da dunt. Instinctively, I unknowingly and tightly gripped the push to talk button on the radio handset and yelled over the radio “Holy sh_t, what is that?” Jabba was stunned at my response to his previous command. I and those around me, could hear his yelling over the radio “what the heck is going on?” The support crews from the two KUN missile boats called us to find out what was going on? All of us in the RHIB where momentarily stunned and in a short trance* "
