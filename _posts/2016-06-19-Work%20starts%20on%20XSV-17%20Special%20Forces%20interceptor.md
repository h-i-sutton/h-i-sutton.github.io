![Flag](/images/flag_ireland.jpg) Safehaven Marine ([Website](http://www.safehavenmarine.com/)) have begun the manufacture of the new XSV 17m wave piercer design. The 60kts boat is a larger (17m / 56ft) version of their **Barracuda** series. A 1:20 scale model has been tested and construction has started on the hull.   

![XSV17](/images/XSV17_CGI.jpg)  

![XSV17](/images/XSV17_build1.jpg)
