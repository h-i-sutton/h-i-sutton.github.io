![](/images/flag_USA.jpg) US Special Operations Command (USSOCOM) comtinue to be surprisingly open about their maritime procurement programs, giving regular updates on the progress of their surface systems, SDV programs and related capabilities. Below is a brief overview. For a more complete update, download their [PDF](http://www.dtic.mil/ndia/2015SOFIC/PEOMaritimeLehnhardt2.pdf.html).  

 **Swimmer (SEAL) Delivery**   
  
a) **Shallow Water Combat Submersible** (SWCS) - prototype under construction with tested expected end 2015. Replaces SEAL Delivery Vehicle (SDV) Mk.8 Mod-1 and will be designated SDV Mk.11. See [article](/US%20Navy%20SEALs%20next%20generation%20SDV%20Mk.XI%20(SWCS).html)  
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM SWCS SDV Mk.11](/images/SWCS_build2.jpg)  
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM SWCS SDV Mk.11](/images/SWCS_build1.jpg)  
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM SWCS SDV Mk.11](/images/SWCS_compare.jpg)  
  

b) **UOES-3** (Button 5.60) Dry Combat Submersible (DCS) aka Dry-SDV - delivered from Italy and entering testing. Beautiful 6-man dry SDV which could in future fit inside an extended Dry Deck Shelter (DDS). See [article]({% post_url 2015-06-22-SEALs%20+%20USSOCOM%20next%20generation%20sub%20UOES3 %})   
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM UOES3 Button 5.60](/images/UOES3.jpg)  
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM UOES3 Button 5.60](/images/UOES3_tall.jpg)  
  

c) **UOES-2** (S351) Dry Combat Submersible (DCS) aka Dry-SDV - delivered from UK and entering testing. Full article pending.   
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM UOES2 S351](/images/UOES2.jpg)  
  

d) **Dry Deck Shelter** (DDS) upgrades to handle larger craft, plus improvements to automation and maintenance.   
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM DDS Dry Deck Shelter](/images/DDS2.jpg)  
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM DDS Dry Deck Shelter](/images/DDS1.jpg)  
*Note that LDUUV is an unmanned submersible program and 'TD' (technology demonstrator) shows the UOES-3*  
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM DDS Dry Deck Shelter](/images/DDS3.jpg)

 **Surface Systems**   
  
a) **Combatant Craft (Assault)** (CCA) - in production.   
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM CCA](/images/CCA.jpg)  
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM CCA](/images/CCA1.jpg)  
*Second photo from* [Good Morning Gloster](https://goodmorninggloucester.wordpress.com).  
  

b) **Combatant Craft Heavy** (CCH) - Sealion-II. - 2 in service with re-production planned.   
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM CCH SEALION](/images/CCH.jpg)  

c) **Combatant Craft Medium** (CCM) - Mk.I. - in production with 16 craft currently budgetted against a requirement for 30.   
![US NAVY SEALS SOCOM USSOCOM CCM](/images/CCM-Mk1a.jpg)
