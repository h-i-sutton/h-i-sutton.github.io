**Newsflash: KANYON**  
An unclassified Nuclear Posture Review document revealed by the [Huffington Post](http://www.huffingtonpost.com.au/2018/01/11/trump-s-nuclear-review-he-wants-a-lot-more-nukes_a_23331446/) contains a tiny reference which confirms the seriousness given to the KANYON nuclear-armed nuclear-powered torpedo.

![Kanyon](/images/KANYON_update1.jpg)  

The document describes KANYON as a new Intercontinental, nuclear armed, undersea autonomous torpedo. It uses the ‘Status-6’ label rather than ‘KANYON’ however.
