![Flag](/images/flag_germany.jpg) Two-man SDVs (Swimmer Delivery Vehicles) built and tested by *Wehrtechnische Dienststelle* WTD-71 (Technical Center for Ships and Naval Weapons, Marine Technology and Research) in Eckemforde.  

WDT-71 developed several two-man wet subs for the Kampfschwimmers, Germanyâ€™s famous Special Forces combat swimmers. In the 1980s the built a small enclosed craft with the crew sitting in tandem. The forward hydroplanes appear to be the same design as the later craft.   
![German WDT-71 SDV](/images/WDT71_a.jpg)  

More recently they devised a very compact side-by-side design with the swimmers laying prone behind large watershields. Several of the craft were built but it did not enter service and two are now used to amuse and confuse visitors to their base. Very little is known about the type in the public sphere.
