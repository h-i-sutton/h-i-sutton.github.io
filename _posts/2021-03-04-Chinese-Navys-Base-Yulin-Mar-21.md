# Incredible New Google Earth Images Of Chinese Nuclear Submarines

![](/images/flag_China.jpg) The latest high-resolution satellite imagery of Yulin on Hainan, South China Sea, shows 7 submarines. One of the boats, a Type-09IV Jin Class SSBN, has some of its missile doors open. Tents are covering some of the open hatches.   

![Chinese submarine on Google Earth, Maxar satellite imagery](/images/China-Submarines-Yulin-Type-093-Shang-class.jpg)

The visible submarines are three Type-09IV Jin Class ballistic missile submarines, two Type-09III Shang Class, one Type-09IIIA Shang-II and a Type-09I Han Class. Other submarines may be present in the [submarine tunnel](http://www.hisutton.com{% post_url 2020-05-07-Submarine-Tunnels %}).
