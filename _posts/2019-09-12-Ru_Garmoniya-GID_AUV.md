[![](/images/Icon_Bluesky.jpg)](https://bsky.app/profile/covertshores.bsky.social)**Russian Autonomous Underwater vehicle Garmoniya-GUIDE**  
![](/images/flag_russia.jpg) The **Project 7Р22 Garmoniya-GUIDE** (Гармония-ГИД, translated as *Harmony GUIDE*) is an unmanned underwater vehicle project in Russia. The vehicle is designed by Rubin and likely fits within the large displacement UUV category (LDUUV). Seven vehicles were expected to be built under a 14’466’013.33 rubles (225,000 USD) contract although the project may have been cancelled in 2018. Either way the design shows Russian UUV evolution, fitting between the [klavesin-2P-PM](http://www.hisutton.com{% post_url 2019-03-30-Harpsichord_AUV %}) (aka Harpsichord-2P-PM) and [Cephalopod](http://www.hisutton.com{% post_url 2018-07-27-Cephalopod %}) armed XLUUV design.   
![Pr. Covert Shores](/images/Ru_Garmoniya-GUIDE_profile.jpg)   
Note the regular slow-turning screw-back propeller (‘screw’) and large vertical rudder with communication pod on the top. This points to a long endurance cruising mode operating relatively near the surface like a regular submarine. Note the fixed horizontal and vertical thruster both forward and aft of what appears to be a payload bay module.
