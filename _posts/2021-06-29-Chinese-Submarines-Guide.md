#Covert Shores Surface Recognition Guide To Chinese Navy Submarines![Flag China](/images/flag_China.jpg) A quick guide to the submarine classes of the People's Liberation Army Navy (PLAN). The chart is intended as a practical guide and does not include every sub-variant.  

These recognition guides were a familiar part of the Cold War. In modern times they have fallen out of favor. However in my experience they are extremely valuable and still have a place in the OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) toolkit.
