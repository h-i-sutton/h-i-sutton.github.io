Photos have emerged of improvised sea mines planted in Mukalla in Yemen by retreating Al-Qaeda in the Arabian Peninsula (AQAP) forces who left the port city in April this year. The mines were spotted by local divers who alerted Hadrami forces.

Article: [southernhirak.org](http://www.southernhirak.org/2016/10/south-yemen-coast-guard-dismantle.html)   

The mines appear to be constructed from two pressurized gas canisters cut in half and welded end-end. The explosive contents and reason why they were not detonated by AQAP have not been revealed.  
![Improvised Sea Mine from AQAP](/images/ISM2.jpg)  
![Improvised Sea Mine from AQAP](/images/ISM3.jpg)  

The mines have been removed by divers from Hadrami Elite Forces Anti-Terrorism Unit and the Coast Guard.
