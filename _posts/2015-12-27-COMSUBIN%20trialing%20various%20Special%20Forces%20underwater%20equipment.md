![](/images/flag_italy.jpg) The Italian Navy has published some very nice photos of COMSUBIN operating various underwater vehicles. None of these types are thought to be operational with the unit, but excellent photography.  

 **Jetboots**   

Diver propulsion device made by Patriot 3, USA. Note the small propulsors attached to the diver's leg:  

Manufacturer's [website](http://www.patriot3.com/maritime-products1/jetboots)  

![Italian COMSUBIN GOI](/images/COMSUBIN5.jpg)  

 **BlackShadow 730**   

Diver propulsion vehicle made by Rotinor, Germany.  

Manufacturer's [website](http://www.rotinor.com/blackshadow-730.html)  

![Italian COMSUBIN GOI](/images/COMSUBIN4.jpg)  

![Italian COMSUBIN GOI](/images/COMSUBIN3.jpg)  

 **DPD**   

Diver propulsion vehicle made by Stidd, USA. This is an early model not the current improved version.  

Manufacturer's [website](http://stiddmil.com/diver-propulsion-device-dpd/)
