#OSINT Analysis Shows That Iran Launched A New Missile Boat![Flag Iran](/images/flag_iran.jpg) While everybody and their mother watches the Iranian Navy's largest warship, Makran, [transit the English Channel](https://news.usni.org/2021/07/18/two-iranian-warships-spotted-near-english-channel), the IRGC has quietly launched one of its new missile corvettes. The boat, which is a large stealthy catamaran design, was launched at a yard on Qeshm Island between June 27 and July 2.   
![Iran Launched A New Missile Boat](/images/Iran-Qeshm-Warship-Launch.jpg)  

The new type of missile boat has been provisionally identified as the Shahid Soleimani-class. Three have been under construction at various shipyards in Iran. They are much larger than preceding IRGC missile boats and represent a step change for their naval force.
