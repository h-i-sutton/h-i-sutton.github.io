# Cutaway of Narco VSV interdicted by US Navy LCS

The USS Gabrielle Giffords, an Independence-class littoral combat ship (LCS)  [interdicted a narco-submarine](https://news.usni.org/2020/12/14/lcs-uss-gabrielle-giffords-nabs-narco-sub-in-the-pacific) in the Pacific Ocean on December 5. The narco sub was a Low Profile Vessel (LPV) which cannot fully submerge. This type uses a Very Slender hull form (VSV). The cutaway shows the internal arrangement of this specific type of narco-VSV.
