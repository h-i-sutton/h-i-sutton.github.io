**Sea-Air-Space 2017**  
![Flag](/images/flag_USA.jpg) The Navy League’s [Sea-Air-Space](http://www.seaairspace.org/) 2017 Exposition has recently finished in Washington DC, USA. There was always a lot of new and interesting underwater developments. **Navy Recognition** have done their usual excellent [reporting](http://www.navyrecognition.com/index.php/news/naval-exhibitions/2017/sea-air-space-2017-show-daily-news.html) complete with a [MUST-WATCH YOUTUBE video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kCIIS8fCtfE). Here are some systems to watch:  

**3-D printed SDV / LDUUV**  
The result of the Print-a-thon technology program, this full-scale model represents a 3D printed SEAL Delivery Vehicle (SDV) and/or optionally crewed Large Displacement Unmanned Underwater Vehicle (LDUUV). It was produced by Naval Sea Systems Command Carderock's Disruptive Technology Lab. The wet submersible was deigned in just 2 weeks!  
![News Round-up April 2017](/images/SAS2017_LDUUV.jpg)  
*Photo from* [shephardmedia](https://www.shephardmedia.com/news/special-operations/sas-2017-3-d-printing-meets-swimmer-delivery/)  

**Boeing Echo Voyager**  
![News Round-up April 2017](/images/SAS2017_EchoVoyager2.jpg)  
Boeing displayed a model of their latest and largest unmanned undersea vehicle (UUV), the 51 ft (16 m) Echo Voyager. Amounting to an unmanned diesel-electric submarine, it can operate for several months at a time thanks to a hybrid rechargeable power system. It has several masts for ISR (Intelligence Surveillance and Reconnaissance) missions and a Modular Payload Bay.   
![News Round-up April 2017](/images/SAS2017_EchoVoyager.jpg)  
![News Round-up April 2017](/images/SAS2017_EchoVoyager1.jpg)  

**SWCS**  
A simplified model of USSOCOM's new SEAL delivery Vehicle (SDV), the Shallow Water Combat Submersible (SWCS) was on display.   

**Taiwan to build four new submarines**  
![News Round-up April 2017](/images/Hai_Lung.jpg)  
*Taiwanese Hai Lung Class SS*  

Taiwan recently announced plans to build four diesel-electric submarine to replace the two Dutch built Hai Lung (Sea Dragon) Class (variant of the Zwaardvis Class) and two much older US built GUPPYs. The move is instead of building a larger fleet of midget submarines per [THIS](http://www.hisutton.com{% post_url 2017-02-16-Taiwanese_midget_sub %}) previous article.  
![News Round-up April 2017](/images/ROCN_midget940.jpg)  

**US Navy Snakehead UUV news**  
![Flag](/images/flag_USA.jpg) USNI News [reports](https://news.usni.org/2017/04/04/navy-splits-lduuv-into-rapid-acquisition-program-at-peo-lcs-rd-effort-at-onr) that the Navy is acelerating work on ‘Snakehead’ Large Displacement Unmanned Underwater Vehicle (LDUUV). The vehicle will equip the Littoral Combat Ships (LCS) and provide Intelligence and Preparation of the Environment (IPOE) and Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (ISR) capabilities.   
![News Round-up April 2017](/images/Snakehead_LDUUV.jpg)
