![Flag](/images/flag_USA.jpg) The Shallow Water Combat Submersible (SWCS) is a program to replace the excellent but ageing SDV M.VIII Mod-1 SEAL Delivery Vehicle.   

![US Navy SEALs next generation SDV (SWCS)](/images/USN_SWCS_render.jpg)   

![US Navy SEALs next generation SDV (SWCS)](/images/USN_SWCS940.jpg)   

The $383m development contract was awarded in 2011 to Teledyne Brown Engineering (against The Columbus Group who built the SDV Mk.VIII Mod-1) with the first test unit delivered in late 2014.
